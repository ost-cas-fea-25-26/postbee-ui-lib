# Borders

These are the border-specific design tokens and utilities used by the library. They establish a consistent vocabulary for border widths, semantic border colors, and corner radii so components (cards, inputs, buttons, separators, etc.) share uniform edge treatments. Use these tokens to ensure predictable visual hierarchy, easier theming, and simpler maintenance across the codebase.
